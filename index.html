BIOGRAPHY OF PRIEST CALEB

"Monsignor Onyekachukwu is highly respected Personality, an icon in the Catholic Church, who has Achieved and contributed wonderfully to the growth of the church in general in and around Okigwe Diocese, including in Nigeria, Africa and to the entire world at large. He has groomed and helped so many in their vocational life, a man of God whose life is worthy of emulation."

"The Founder of St. Peter's Seminary Okigwe, Monsignor Onyekachukwu Luke, cannot be forgotten in a hurry because of his immense contribution towards the development of God's Vineyard. A dedicated servant of God, who is known as a peace maker, has touched many lives through his preaching of the word of God which has restored hope to many People's lives. "

"Msgr.Onyekachukwu's death has been described as a huge lost to the Diocese and and to the entire Catholic Church at large. He is a native of Arondizuogu in Imo State of Nigeria. He was born in year 1935 and died in year 2020 at the age of 83 years. He will be remembered by many through his simplicity life."

Eternal rest Grant unto him oh Lord and Let your perpetual light shine upon him, my the soul of Monsignor Onyekachukwu Luke and the souls of all the faithful departed through the mercy of God rest in peace. Amen.
